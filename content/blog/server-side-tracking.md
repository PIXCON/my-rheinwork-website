If your reports feel less trustworthy than they used to, you are not imagining it.
Ad blockers, privacy settings and browser changes all quietly chip away at classic,
browser-based tracking.

## Where the data is collected matters

Server-side tracking moves the collection of events from the visitor's browser to your
own server. Instead of relying on a script that may be blocked, your application records
what happened and forwards it on its own terms.

The result is data that is more complete, more consistent, and easier to keep clean.

## It is infrastructure, not a plugin

Done well, this is not a checkbox you tick once. It is a small piece of infrastructure:
clear events, sensible naming, and a foundation you can actually trust your decisions on.

That is exactly how we treat it — measurement as engineering, not an afterthought.
