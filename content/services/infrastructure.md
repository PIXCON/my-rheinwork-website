Software doesn't just have to be built — it has to be operated, and that's where everyday
reliability is decided. Reliability isn't luck; it's the result of disciplined routines: automated
deployments, sensible monitoring and an environment prepared for the worst case.

We set up your infrastructure cleanly and keep it running: **cloud setup, Docker and — where it
makes sense — Kubernetes**, plus **CI/CD pipelines** for smooth delivery and **monitoring** that
fires on what really matters instead of crying wolf. Hosting is on **EU servers in Germany**.

Our principle: only as much technology as needed. Not every project needs a complex cluster — we
choose the setup that fits your project instead of spinning up expensive tools for their own sake.
