SEO doesn't start with content, it starts with the foundation. If a page loads slowly, renders
poorly or is technically messy, even the best copy won't help. We take care of exactly this
technical layer: the bedrock every good ranking builds on. Content and link building we deliberately
leave to others; our strength is the technology.

### Speed is a ranking factor

Loading time is measurable and an official ranking factor. Faster pages rank better, keep visitors
longer and noticeably lift conversion. We optimise loading times, rendering and image files far
enough that your site competes at the top, instead of giving away enquiries through sluggish
technology. Whatever can be improved in your [Core Web Vitals]({{< relref "/blog/hugo-website-performance" >}})
scores, we get it out.

### Provable Core Web Vitals

We aim for green scores, up to 100/100 in technical SEO. More important than the number is that it's
provable: we measure before and after and show you exactly what changed. A vague promise becomes a
result you can verify and pin your success to.

### Rendering and JavaScript SEO

Modern, JavaScript-heavy sites often fail to show search engines all of their content reliably. We
make sure your pages are rendered cleanly, crawled fully and indexed correctly, and we make them
additionally understandable through structured data. That way Google sees what your visitors see,
without gaps.

### Measurably better, not just claimed

We work data- and fact-driven: measure first, prioritise by impact, then implement. Classic content
marketing or link building is deliberately not what we do. We're not an agency that sells backlinks,
but engineers who bring the technical basis of your visibility to top level, with individual
strategies per project.
