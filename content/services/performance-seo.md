SEO doesn't start with content — it starts with the foundation. If a page loads slowly, renders
poorly or is technically messy, even the best copy won't help. We take care of exactly this
technical layer — the bedrock every good ranking builds on.

Concretely that means: getting **Core Web Vitals** into the green (we aim for top scores up to
100/100), solving **rendering** and JavaScript SEO cleanly, optimising **loading times** and
**image files**, and making the page understandable to search engines via **structured data**. We
work data- and fact-driven: measure first, prioritise by impact, then implement — and the result is
provable.

What we deliberately **don't** do: classic content marketing or link building. We're not an SEO
agency that sells backlinks, but engineers who bring the technical basis of your visibility to top
level — with individual strategies per project. As Google-certified specialists, we know what counts.
