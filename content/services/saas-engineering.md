A SaaS product stands or falls on its foundation. We build your product from the first lean MVP to a
scalable platform, cleanly structured and laid out so it grows with you, instead of needing a rebuild
after the first success.

### Start lean, build solid

A good [MVP]({{< relref "/blog/mvp-without-cutting-corners" >}}) is small in scope but uncompromising
in its foundation. We cut features, not quality, so you reach the market fast and learn early what
your customers really need. The first version holds, and the next ten features build on it, without
the platform being created from scratch.

### Multi-tenant from day 1

As soon as several customers run on one platform, the architecture decides on security and
maintainability. We build a clean multi-tenant architecture in which your customers' data is reliably
separated. Retrofitting this is one of the most expensive rebuilds of all, which is why we factor it
in from the start where it makes sense.

### Billing and user management that hold

Recurring revenue needs reliable workflows. Via Stripe we handle subscriptions, invoices and
self-service, so users sign up, upgrade and cancel on their own. Considered user management with
roles and permissions, and the right admin interfaces, give you control over your product without
every change going through development.

### Ready to grow

Growth should not be a risk. We lay out the data model and architecture to hold when ten customers
become a thousand, and prepare deliberately for higher loads instead of over-engineering across the
board. And we don't just talk about SaaS, we build it ourselves. We know the path from idea to
running product from our own experience, including every lever that matters in practice.
