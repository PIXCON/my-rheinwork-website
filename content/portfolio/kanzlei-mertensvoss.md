**The brief.** A corporate law firm wins mandates on reputation and discretion, long before a contract is signed. Mertens & Voß needed a presence that signals decades of standing and discreet competence to boards, founders and general counsel — authoritative without ever raising its voice.

**The approach.** A deep navy canvas with restrained gold accents sets a classic, serious tone, while the Prata serif lends the editorial gravitas of a firm that has been consulted since 1987. The page is structured as a quiet argument: a three-word credo, a clear practice-area grid, and named partners who put faces to the firm's competence.

**Why it works.** Every decision reassures a risk-averse, high-stakes audience: legible structure over marketing copy, real partners over stock imagery, and a calm, confidential contact path. Trust here is built through restraint and clarity.
