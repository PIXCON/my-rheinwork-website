**The brief.** An AI fitness product needs a launch site that feels like the app itself — fast, kinetic and premium — and converts athletes into downloads. The bar was top-tier mobile-app-startup polish, not a template.

**The approach.** Matte black, a single electric neon-green accent and a heavy Archivo / Bebas display stack give it an unmistakable athletic energy. A live phone mockup with floating metric cards shows the product working right in the hero, while count-up stats, an animated analytics chart and an AI-coach mockup carry the story from feature to feature.

**Why it works.** Every section is engineered to convert: instant social proof (2.4M athletes, 4.9★), a tangible product preview, and a clear path to the app store — wrapped in motion and detail that signal a brand worth trusting with your training.
