**The brief.** A Fear-Free veterinary practice needed a website that felt less like a clinic intake form and more like a trusted friend. The goal was to lower anxiety before owners ever set foot inside, while making the appointment and the 24h emergency line impossible to miss.

**The approach.** We built around the Fear-Free idea itself, leading with a species selection so dogs, cats, small pets and exotics each get their own calm path. Warm cream, clay and a grounding sage band carry the philosophy section, Lora's editorial serif lends the reassuring, almost human voice, and a Ratgeber guide answers the worries owners are too nervous to ask aloud.

**Why it works.** Every section earns trust before it asks for a booking: species-specific entry points, a clear care philosophy, and emergency access that is always one tap away. The result reads like a practice that genuinely understands the animal in the room, and the booking flow converts because the worry was handled first.
