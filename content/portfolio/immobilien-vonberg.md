**The brief.** Luxury real estate is sold on emotion and discretion, not on listing portals. Von Berg needed a presence that feels like the homes it represents — cinematic, calm and exclusive — while quietly doing the work of generating qualified seller and buyer enquiries.

**The approach.** A near-black, cinematic canvas lets the architectural photography breathe, while a refined Didone serif (Bodoni Moda) signals heritage and taste. The page is structured as a discreet journey: a curated portfolio, a confidential "sell off-market" path, and an understated proof of credibility — never loud, always premium.

**Why it works.** Every element reassures a high-net-worth audience: large, restrained imagery, price-on-request where appropriate, and a 48-hour valuation hook that converts owners without feeling like a portal. Trust here is built through understatement.
