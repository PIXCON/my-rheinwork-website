**The brief.** A modern treasury and cash-management platform needed a landing page that speaks to corporate finance teams without the cold, jargon-heavy feel of legacy banking software. The challenge: convey real-time control over global cash, liquidity and risk — and earn the confidence of a CFO — in the very first scroll.

**The approach.** We chose a light, institutional canvas with a deep navy foundation, a calm green accent and a touch of gold for warmth. A live global cash-position dashboard — area chart plus FX tiles — anchors the hero so the core value is visible immediately, while Onest paired with IBM Plex Mono keeps figures precise and the tone refined. The page then builds a measured argument: the cash position, a step-by-step approval workflow, bank and ERP connectivity, and a treasurer's testimonial backed by hard numbers.

**Why it works.** Every section answers a question a finance buyer actually asks — *Where is my cash? Who approved this payment? Does it connect to my banks and ERP? Has it worked for someone like me?* The restrained palette and generous spacing make a dense, high-stakes subject feel controlled and reassuring, exactly the temperament a treasury team wants from the system that runs their money.
