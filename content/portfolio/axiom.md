**The brief.** A modern data-infrastructure platform needs a landing page that earns the trust of a sceptical, technical audience — without a single price tag and without the generic SaaS template look. The goal: communicate speed, scale and credibility in the first scroll.

**The approach.** We led with a *working product*, not a promise. A near-black canvas with a violet-to-cyan accent keeps it unmistakably engineering-grade, while a live SQL query mock in the hero proves the core value instantly. From there the page builds a careful argument — capabilities, architecture, a head-to-head comparison and social proof — pacing the reader from curiosity to "book a demo."

**Why it works.** Every section answers a real buyer question: *Is it fast? Does it lock me in? How does it fit my stack? Who else trusts it?* Modern typography (Manrope paired with IBM Plex Mono) and generous, calm spacing make a dense, technical story feel effortless to read.
